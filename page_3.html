What is our Mission?

Our Mission Here at the Maize and Blue Cupboarda is to ensure members of the University of Michigan community—whether on a tight budget or physically restrained from getting to a grocery store—receive equitable access to healthy, nutritious, and nourishing food and the ability to prepare it for themselves or others.

Here at the Maize and Blue Cupboard we have a lot of resources that anyone of all ages can use whether it be utensils or groceries! We restock our supplies every Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday! Come make an appointment now, take as much groceries as you need but make sure to leave some for others too! Make sure to arrive on time for your appointment because spots are limited!!
